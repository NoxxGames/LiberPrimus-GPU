# Star-Like Compression Artefact Policy

Star-like structures, block boundaries, bitplane oddities, and residual patterns are review observations only.

## Required Controls

Before any future interpretation:

- compare source-locked image variants;
- record exact source URL/path/hash/dimensions;
- include ordinary JPEG and non-JPEG negative controls;
- define metrics before looking for a target pattern;
- keep generated visualisations ignored;
- keep `usable_as_experiment_seed=false` until a later review/promotion record explicitly changes it.

## Prohibited Shortcuts

- Do not treat compression metrics as hidden-message evidence.
- Do not infer page text, symbols, or cipher routes from residual patterns.
- Do not use OCR, AI, or ML as source truth.
- Do not commit raw images or generated derived images.
- Do not promote image artefact observations to executable manifests during preflight.

Stage 4M is intentionally boring: it makes future comparison reproducible without making meaning claims.
