# Crib Checks

Stage 3C adds a tiny transparent crib list at `data/scoring/cribs-tiny-v0.txt`.

The list is intentionally small and contains generic or known topic terms such as `LIBER`, `PRIMUS`, `CICADA`, and `WISDOM`.

Crib hits are weak triage features only. They do not prove plaintext correctness, page boundaries, corpus canonical status, or a solve.

Do not tune scoring to make a specific candidate look good. Do not replace the tiny crib list with a large phrase list without a future explicit stage and null-control update.
