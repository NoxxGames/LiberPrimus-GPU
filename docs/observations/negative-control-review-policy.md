# Negative Control Review Policy

Negative controls are first-class outputs. They are not failed paperwork and do
not become evidence of hidden meaning.

Stage 4J preserves false-positive classes such as braille overfitting,
constellation overfitting, forced 13/31 dot readings, cuneiform-as-fact,
broad stego brute force, spectrogram pareidolia, and AI-generated solves.

Negative controls may be used as controls in future manifests. They must not be
treated as accepted claims, canonical observations, or seed evidence.
