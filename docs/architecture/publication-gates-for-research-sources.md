# Publication Gates For Research Sources

Stage 5AL records these publication states:

- `metadata_only_safe`
- `private_deep_research_only`
- `generated_extract_review_required`
- `blocked_private_or_sensitive`
- `raw_source_never_publish`
- `public_website_review_required`
- `external_link_only`

Raw third-party files are never publishable by default. Generated extracts remain private
Deep Research inputs unless reviewed. Community-facts message bodies, private IDs, private
URLs, and raw attachment bodies are blocked.

Source cards and claim metadata may be used by future website tooling only after a review
step confirms that they contain no private text, private identifiers, or raw source bodies.
