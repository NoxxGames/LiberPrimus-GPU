# Historical Technique Taxonomy

Stage 5BF groups historical Cicada route artefacts into authenticity, stego, encoding, numeric, text-reference, network-route, physical-route, and Liber Primus categories.

The taxonomy is a review aid for Stage 5BG. A technique count means local source-lock metadata exists, not that the technique was executed or that it should be used in token-block planning.

Future token-block planning must treat these records as constraints and control-design inputs until Deep Research reviews their relevance.
