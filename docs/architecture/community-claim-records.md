# Community Claim Records

Stage 5AK adds a metadata-only claim-record layer for the local community-facts folder at `third_party/UsefulFilesAndIdeas/community-facts/`.

Community facts are claim records, not truth records. A claim record preserves the claim family, source locator, ordered image references, formula text, input entities, claimed values, correction status, risk level, source-lock needs, null-control needs, and publication policy. It does not make a claim execution-ready.

The Stage 5AK records deliberately separate:

- arithmetic preflight that can be checked from explicit integers;
- source material that still needs exact transcript or image-source policy;
- label or count policies that need human review;
- null and multiple-testing controls required before any future verifier.

Public website publication is blocked until a later review stage. Private Deep Research handoffs may preserve technical formulas and message context in ignored local body files. No OCR, AI/ML interpretation, image forensics, stego/audio execution, CUDA, benchmarks, scored experiments, hypothesis execution, corpus activation, page-boundary finalisation, method-status upgrade, or solve claim is part of this layer.
