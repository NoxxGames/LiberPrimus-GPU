# Stage 5AK Community Facts Integration

Stage 5AK is not an experiment. It integrates a local Discord/forum-derived community-facts folder into the source-harvester metadata system.

Committed outputs include local inventory metadata, ordered attachment metadata, source-card and content-index summaries, clue-category records, claim records, correction logs, arithmetic-preflight metadata, website-ingest metadata, Deep Research pack metadata, readiness records, guardrails, next-stage decision records, and an aggregate summary.

Generated reports and private body files remain ignored. The raw text log and raw image attachments remain under `third_party/UsefulFilesAndIdeas/community-facts/` and are not committed.

No hypothesis test, source crawling, OCR, AI/ML interpretation, image forensics, stego/audio execution, CUDA, benchmark, scored experiment, corpus activation, page-boundary finalisation, method-status upgrade, website expansion, or solve claim is performed.
