# Stage 3Y Result Synthesis And Method Retirement

Stage 3Y converts the accumulated Stage 0-3 history into durable project memory. It does not execute experiments, process raw data, change CLI behaviour, use CUDA, activate the canonical corpus, finalise page boundaries, or make a solve claim.

## Outputs

- `docs/roadmap/staged-plan.md` records completed stages, current direction, planned stages, deferred work, retired/deprioritised directions, and Deep Research influence.
- `data/research/stage-summary-records-v0.yaml` records stage-family summaries.
- `data/research/method-family-status-records-v0.yaml` records method family status.
- `data/research/method-retirement-records-v0.yaml` records retirement/deprioritisation guardrails.
- `data/research/deep-research-influence-records-v0.yaml` records Deep Research influence.
- `data/research/project-direction-change-records-v0.yaml` records direction changes and affected docs.

## Result Synthesis

Most Stage 3A-3J text/numeric experiments were noisy or inconclusive. Stage 3S Onion 7 explicit seed execution was inconclusive. Stage 3U cookie SHA-256 packs found zero exact matches. Stage 3T GP/rune claim verification was useful because it recomputed exact claims and quarantined unsupported ones. Stage 3V OutGuess work produced infrastructure, but historical fixtures and local tool setup remain future work.

## Method-Retirement Policy

Noisy, negative, or inconclusive families are not permanently banned from research, but they are blocked from widening unless a new public/source-locked fact constrains the method. Reopening requires updating both the staged plan and the method-retirement ledger.
