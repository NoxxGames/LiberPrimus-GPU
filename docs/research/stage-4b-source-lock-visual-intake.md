Stage 4B converts Stage 4A/Deep Research leads into durable project memory. It promotes selected public sources, records source-health metadata, adds review-only visual/numeric observations, preserves negative controls, and queues disabled future manifests.

- Added Stage 4B source-lock triage schemas and `libreprimus source-lock-triage`.

## Evidence Policy

Stage 4B evidence is classified into source-lock targets, review-only observations, negative controls, duplicate/noisy links, unsafe/private links, and disabled future experiment candidates. Discord/site-derived leads remain hypotheses until public-source corroboration or exact artefact records support promotion.
