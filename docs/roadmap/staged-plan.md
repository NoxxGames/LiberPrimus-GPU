At minimum, check `STATUS.md`, `ROADMAP.md`, `AGENTS.md`, `README.md`, `EXPERIMENTS.md`, `RESULTS_SCHEMA.md`, `TESTING.md`, `CIPHER_CATALOG.md`, this file, tutorials, and `docs/wiki-source`.
