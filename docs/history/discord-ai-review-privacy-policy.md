# Discord AI Review Privacy Policy

Stage 3Q exists so local Discord research material can be reviewed without uploading a 500 MB raw archive or exposing private chat metadata.

## Required Redactions

Generated AI-review bundles must remove or avoid:

- raw Discord HTML logs
- full raw conversation threads
- usernames
- user IDs
- message IDs
- avatar URLs
- private Discord CDN URLs and query strings
- personal chatter not needed for Liber Primus research

Public external URLs may be preserved when they are useful source-discovery leads.

## Allowed Review Content

Redacted topic shards may include:

- public source links
- attachment filenames or extensions, without private URLs
- method keyword clusters
- numeric observations
- visual observation keywords
- false-positive and debunk notes
- suggested next review actions

## Not Evidence

Discord review bundles do not prove a claim. They only make local review more tractable. Any useful lead must be independently checked, source-locked, and promoted through the existing reviewable source/observation registry before future experiments can use it.

## Prohibited Workflows

- Uploading raw Discord logs to AI services.
- Publishing raw Discord logs.
- Calling Discord APIs or scraping Discord.
- Treating Discord claims as facts.
- Treating generated shards as canonical corpus.
- Turning review leads directly into cipher seeds.
