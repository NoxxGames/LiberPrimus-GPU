# Stage 5AR Case Policy Summary

Stage 5AR records case/glyph ambiguity as review metadata and keeps the Stage 5AP canonical transcription unchanged.

The case policy records 9 ambiguity classes and leaves all 9 unresolved pending human review. The records include common visual confusions such as `I/l`, `O/0`, `f/F`, `S/5`, `B/8`, `Z/2`, `G/6`, `A/4`, and `C/G`.

The policy is a null-control and review aid only. It does not authorize alternate-token execution, decode attempts, hash/preimage search, OCR, AI/ML image interpretation, or solve claims.
