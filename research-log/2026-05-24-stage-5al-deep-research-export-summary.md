# Stage 5AL Deep Research Export Summary

Stage 5AL created a private Deep Research export manifest at
`data/source-harvester/stage5al-deep-research-export.yaml`.

The export points to ignored private helper files under `research-inputs/stage5al/` and
records bundle order, source-card count, claim count, missing-source count, publication
gates, do-not-assume notes, known questions, and citation policy.

Deep Research is recommended next only after using this export package. It should not consume
raw `third_party/` paths directly.
