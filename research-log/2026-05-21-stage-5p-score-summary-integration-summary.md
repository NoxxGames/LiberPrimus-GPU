# Stage 5P Score-Summary Integration Summary

Stage 5P wrote five Stage 4I-compatible score-summary integration records over the compact CUDA
parity metadata. The records use `scoring_not_available` with `triage_only` interpretation because
Stage 5P adds no scorer, no calibration profile, and no score-derived evidence.

Score labels remain comparison metadata only. They are not plaintext evidence and cannot upgrade any
method family to solved.
