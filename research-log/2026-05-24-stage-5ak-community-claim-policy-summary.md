# Stage 5AK Community Claim Policy Summary

Community-facts entries are claim records, not truth records. Stage 5AK requires each claim to preserve source context, formula/input/output fields, correction status, risk level, source-lock needs, null-control needs, execution status, and publication status.

All Stage 5AK claims are `execution_ready=false`, `solve_claim=false`, and `website_publication_allowed=false`. High-risk number-fact families require exact transcript/profile sources, image-source or coordinate policies where applicable, and null/multiple-testing controls before any later verifier could be considered.

Arithmetic preflight checks only explicit integer arithmetic, prime-index, Euler-phi, and base-conversion facts. It does not scan transcripts, images, or generated outputs.
