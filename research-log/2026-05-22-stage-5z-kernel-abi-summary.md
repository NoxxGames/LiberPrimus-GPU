# Stage 5Z Kernel ABI Summary

The Stage 5Z kernel ABI names a future `prime_minus_one_stream_candidate_kernel` and records CUDA-C style buffer contracts. It forbids STL, exceptions, RTTI, lambdas, dynamic allocation, and host-side convenience abstractions in CUDA-facing device paths.

No `.cu` or `.cuh` files were changed by Stage 5Z.
