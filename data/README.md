# data

## Purpose

Holds future corpus-related directories and policy notes.

## What belongs here

Raw evidence placeholders, normalized corpus placeholders, solved fixture placeholders, small fixture policy files, and future lock metadata.

## What does not belong here

Generated experiment outputs, databases, logs, notebooks with outputs, or unreviewed downloads.

## Codex modification policy

Codex may modify README and placeholder files. Raw evidence must not be modified in place.

## Stage 0A restrictions

Stage 0A creates directories only. No real corpus data is imported.
