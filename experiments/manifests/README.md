# experiments/manifests

## Purpose

YAML experiment manifests.

## What belongs here

Reviewed manifests that define hypothesis, corpus slice, transforms, scorers, controls, hardware needs, and output policy.

## What does not belong here

Generated outputs, raw data, or candidate result dumps.

## Codex modification policy

Codex may add or update manifests with explicit scope.

## Stage 0A restrictions

Manifests must be smoke or policy-only. No real cryptanalysis runs are defined.
